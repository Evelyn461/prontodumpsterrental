Frequently Asked Questions

What size dumpster should I rent?

Choose a size based on your waste amount: 10-12 yards for small jobs, 15-20 yards for mid-projects, 30-40 yards for large build-outs.

What materials are prohibited?

Hazardous wastes like paint, chemicals, tires, batteries and asbestos are not accepted.

Do I need a permit?

If placing the dumpster on public property like a street, a local permit may be needed. Private driveways usually do not require one.

Can the dumpster damage my property?

Potentially yesdriveways and lawns may be affected. Use protective boards or choose a hard surface.

How is cost determined?

Factors include size, rental duration, debris type, and weight. Always ask for upfront all-inclusive quotes.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Typical rental periods range 7-10 days, with extensions available.
